Inferno

Canto I

When I had journeyed half of our life's way, I found myself within a shadowed forest, for I had lost the path that does not stray.

Canto III

Through me the way to the suffering city; Through me the everlasting pain; Through me the way that runs among the Lost. Justice urged on my exalted Creator: Divine Power made me, The Supreme Wisdom and the Primal Love. Nothing was made before me but eternal things And I endure eternally. Abandon all hope - You Who Enter Here.

Here one must leave behind all hesitation; here every cowardice must meet its death.

Canto VIII

How long in female the fire of love lasts, If the eye or the touch often does not catch up

Canto X

Do you not know that we are worms and born To form the angelic butterfly that soars, Without defenses, to confront His judgment?

Canto XXI

O human race, born to fly upward, wherefore at a little wind dost thou so fall?

Canto XXXIII

Pure and disposed to mount unto the stars.

Paradiso

Canto I

A great flame follows a little spark.

Canto V

The greatest gift which God in His bounty bestowed in creating, and the most conformed to His own goodness and that which He most prizes, was the freedom of the will,with which the creatures that have intelligence, they all and they alone, were and are endowed.

Canto XVII

Fate's arrow, when expected, travels slow.

Canto XXXIII

I saw within Its depth how It conceives all things in a single volume bound by Love, of which the universe is the scattered leaves.

Canto XXXIII

But now was turning my desire and will, Even as a wheel that equally is moved, The Love which moves the sun and the other stars.
